About

Former Product Owner switching career to become a Web Developer.

Since October I’m studying a YH-education at Chas Academy - Fullstack Web Developer. The education gives me good knowledge in, among other things, frontend, backend, programming methodology, UX and more.

This spring, I have built web applications using libraries / frameworks such as React.JS, Angular and Laravel and worked on both my own projects and group assignments.

One reason I applied for this education is the breadth: being able to work on and have an overview of all the parts in a project. And with some school projects behind me, I really feel I have found the right path.

I think it's fun to work with both frontend and backend development. Due to my aesthetic background, I have an interest in UX and design, which has made it easier for me to take on the frontend parts. It is exciting that what you build meets a user and that you work to make it the best experience. At the same time, I find it very exciting to see what the user does not see, and look forward to a deeper understanding of the backend.

In addition to coding in my spare time, I have a big interest in movies and love to watch movies and documentaries. I am interested in art and when I have the opportunity I like to go to exhibitions. Cooking is fun and I try to eat well and exercise.

Background

I’m originally from Karlskrona and have lived in Stockholm since 2011. My interest in working with the web first grew when I read a course in Media Technology at Södertörn University. When I was done, I applied for a job as Tech Stack Manager at Bonnier Magazines & Brands. In the spring of 2019, I was offered the position as acting product owner and worked with two web applications.
